WELCOME TO JECADO HOSTEL

JECADO HOSTEL, OFF-CAMPUS

JECADO hostel was establish in 2015/2016 academic year. The hostel is lacated on the engineering gate high street, heading upward to Kotei.

The hostel is known for its siren environment, which many student proclaim home away from home. More and More, the hostel has one of the best security system which include CCTV cameras, and well equiped security personel.

ROOMS

JECADO Hostel has one of the best affordable standard rooms off-campus. The rooms are very consucive and befits contemporary rooms, kitchen, and washroom settings. The rooms are made up of one in a room and two in a room only.

Also, the rooms are reasonabily spacious which allows residents to have enough space to keep their extra luggages beside the provided wardrobes.

BUILDING

The hostel building structure boost of modern architectural design which is made up of apartments and all other rooms are self contained. Again with stores attached making the building structure a complete estate on it own.
